Features

Gaze tracking

MediaPipe-powered face landmarks detect exactly where you're looking in real time.

Blink to type

Long blink selects a key. Double blink deletes. Short blinks scroll the keyboard.

Text to speech

When you close the keyboard, your typed text is automatically spoken aloud.

Auto-calibration

Eye preview screen auto-tunes blink threshold to your eyes before you start typing.

Runs offline

After the first-run model download, everything works completely offline. No cloud needed.

Windows native

Single .exe, no install required. Just download and double-click to launch.

How it works

1

Launch the app

Double-click OptiLink.exe. On first run it downloads the face landmark model (~29MB).

2

Calibrate your eyes

A preview window shows your eye direction live. Look around and blink a few times — the app auto-tunes to you.

3

Type with your gaze

Look left/right to scroll the keyboard. Hover over a letter and hold a long blink to select it.

4

Hear your message

Close the keyboard and your full typed text is read aloud using the built-in text-to-speech engine.
